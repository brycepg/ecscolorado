Emergency Computer Services is an onsite and in-house tech support company in business in Colorado Springs since 1995. We support computers running Microsoft operating systems, from Windows 95 through Windows 7, and all flavors in between. We also support Apple computers and their operating systems along with other unix-based operating systems like Linux.

Our employees have been in the tech support business since 1985.

In store, we carry a variety of typical computer accessories and components such as power supplies, wireless cards, keyboards, mice, routers and any cable you might need.
